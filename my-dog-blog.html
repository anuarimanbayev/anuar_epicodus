My Dog Tarkhan!!!

Tarkhan is a very rare Kazakh greyhound breed, the Tazy!

The Tazy is historically bred on the Central Asian steppes of Kazakhstan to hunt rabbits, foxes, and even wolves!

Tarkhan's First Real Hunt!

  • Blog Post #2
  • Date: June 15, 2013

Today was a glorious day for a well-muscled Tazy Tarkhan! He led his brother Turkhan in the hunt. Over the course of 3 days hunting, He nabbed 5 rabbits, 2 foxes, and 1 wolf! Wow! Incredible! In contrast, poor Turkhan just nabbed 3 rabbits and 1 fox. Still, Turkhan his brother was vital in bringing down the wolf together. Go go Tazy greyhound teamwork!

Tarkhan is born!

  • Blog Post #1
  • Date: April 20, 2000

Today was a very auspicious day for young Tazy Tarkhan! He was born today alongside his brother Turkhan. They are two healthy lil greyhound cubs! Yet already Tarkhan is the more active of the two.
